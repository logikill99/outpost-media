About Formula 2

F2 is the official feeder series to Formula 1. Every team runs the same chassis (the Dallara F2 2024) and engine (a Mecachrome V6), so results come down to driver craft, race engineering, and strategy execution. Most current F1 drivers — Verstappen, Leclerc, Norris, Piastri, Russell, Hamilton — graduated through F2 or its predecessor (GP2).

Weekend Format

  • Friday: 30-min Free Practice + Qualifying (single 30-min session sets the grid)
  • Saturday: Sprint Race — reverse top-10 grid, no mandatory pit stop, ~25 min + 1 lap
  • Sunday: Feature Race — full grid, mandatory pit stop, ~45 min + 1 lap

Points are awarded across both races, with bonus points for pole position and fastest lap.

Why Watch

The 2026 F2 champion will likely be in F1 by 2027. Watching F2 is essentially scouting the next generation. With Ferrari, Red Bull, Mercedes, McLaren, and Williams all running junior drivers across the F2 grid, every weekend doubles as an interview for an F1 seat.
