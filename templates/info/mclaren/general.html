About the Series

McLaren Trophy America is a one-make championship for the McLaren Artura Trophy EVO — a race-prepared version of McLaren's Artura road car. Now in its second season after launching in 2025, the championship secured a prestigious F1 support slot.

The Car — Artura Trophy EVO

  • Powertrain: 3.0L twin-turbo V6 + e-motor (hybrid), ~585 hp combined
  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch with electric reverse
  • Chassis: Carbon fibre monocoque (MCLA platform)
  • Weight: ~1,395 kg
  • Differentiator: One of very few hybrid one-make cars on a North American grid

Format

  • Two races per weekend
  • 40 minutes + 1 lap each
  • Mandatory pit stop in Race 2 only
  • Single qualifying session sets both grids (Race 2 grid based on Race 1 fastest lap)
