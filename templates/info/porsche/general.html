About the Series

PCCNA is a one-make championship — every competitor races the same car: the Porsche 911 GT3 Cup. No development allowed, no manufacturer spending wars. Just drivers, identical machinery, and pure racecraft. The series runs alongside major North American motorsport events including F1 and IMSA.

The Car — 992.2 (new for 2026)

  • Engine: 4.0L flat-six naturally aspirated, ~520 hp
  • Gearbox: 6-speed Porsche sequential
  • Aero: Updated front splitter, rear wing, and underbody
  • New for 2026: Air conditioning (matters in event-day heat), revised electronics, refined ABS
  • Tires: Michelin Pilot Sport Cup spec
